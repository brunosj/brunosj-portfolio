I am a musician and DJ based in Berlin, Germany. I am part of a small record label, Pinewax Records, where we produce original music, organise events, and release different types of musical content (mixtapes, livesets, radio shows, etc.). I also compose music for film soundtracks and various other media projects. Additionally, I am a data enthusiast and am currently exploring ways to connect the world of data/AI and music.
